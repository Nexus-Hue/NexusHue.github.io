Moving to California

After moving to California to live with his mom, Nexus‚òÖHue continued sketching anime and cartoons until 6th grade. High School

Nexus‚òÖHue began making videos again, combining skateboarding with his editing skills to create unconventional comedic edits. He would make commercials for odd products. In 2016, Nexus‚òÖHue attended Fresno State University as an undeclared major but dropped out after one semester due to the stress of balancing school and finances.
